Fashion Store

The different types of modeling used in fashion designing include sketching, draping, flat pattern making, and 3D modeling.
Each of these techniques has its unique advantages and is used depending on the designer's preference and the requirements of the client.
Fashion models should also have an outgoing personality and superb people skills, since they will usually need to interact with a variety of different people while they are working.
They may need to interact with stylists, makeup artists, designers, and photographers.
